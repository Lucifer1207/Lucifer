HARDWIRED CONTROL UNIT &
MICRO-PROGRAMMED CONTROL UNIT....

HARDWIRED

*Depend Upon Hardware.
*Used By RISC.
*Diagnose Is Hard.
*Complex Structure.
*No Use Of Memory.
*difficulty occurs In complex instructions.
*Fast Speed.
*No AI's And Technology Used.
*Non-Reprogrammable.
*Software Can't Be Updated.

MICRO-PROGRAMMED

*Depend Upon Software.
*Used By CISC.
*Diagnose Is Easy.
*Simple Structure.
*Memory Is Used.
*No difficulty occurs In complex instructions.
*Slow Speed.
*AI's And Technology Are Both Used.
*Reprogrammable.
*Software Can Be Updated.
